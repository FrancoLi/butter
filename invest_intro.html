Investing With Us

Micro Asset-Based Loans (MABL)

Unlike common investments, micro asset-based lending is a unique investment product that offers high-returns and strong lender protection. Each loan is backed by a collateral and the value is verifed by a professional appraiser.

Institutional investors and banks have long invested and structured transactions like these on a larger scale, and now everyone can participate!

Target Annual Returns of Up to 24%

We target investments that have high yields and are risk-adjusted by structure, passing-through the returns to the ultimate investor.

Diversification

Our investment products are unlike traditional equity and fixed-income securities. Each transaction provides investors an alternative investment in their portfoio.

Low Equity Market Correlation

There is low correlation with the global equity markets, as each transaction is unique with a distinctive borrower and risk.

Lender Protection

All investments are extensively vetted before offered, and every transaction has strong credit enhancements favoring the investor.

Aligned Interest

[Butter] not only acts as the servicer on these transactions, but will co-invest in every offering, facing the same risks as investors.

Limited Duration

Investments are expected to be short-term, limited to 12-months financing, irrespective of collateral.

Monitoring

All investors can monitor their performance through the platform. A monthly remittance and statement will be generated for each investor.
